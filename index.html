Early Life and Education

Jeffrey Preston Bezos was born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He graduated from Princeton University in 1986 with degrees in electrical engineering and computer science.

Career

Amazon

In 1994, Bezos founded Amazon as an online bookstore. The company expanded into various sectors, becoming the world's largest online retailer and a prominent cloud computing provider through Amazon Web Services.

Blue Origin

In 2000, Bezos founded Blue Origin, an aerospace manufacturer and sub-orbital spaceflight services company. In 2021, he flew into space aboard Blue Origin's New Shepard vehicle.

The Washington Post

In 2013, Bezos purchased The Washington Post for $250 million, revitalizing the newspaper's digital presence.

Wealth

As of May 2025, Jeff Bezos's estimated net worth stood at $220.9 billion, making him the third richest individual in the world.
